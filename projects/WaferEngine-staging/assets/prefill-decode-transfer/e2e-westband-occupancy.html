Confirmed: no free seam→edge lane. The HT band (x≈3–131) is occupied on every row — decode HT (y1–512), the logits-mux + pf-demux at the seam rows (y513–514), and prefill HT (y515–1026). What actually works — and the fix

Piggyback the logits egress (device-proven). The working logits stream originates inside the wall — ht_tail at x≈3 hops straight to the io_port at x=0 (no crossing), and it already carries an 8-u32 TSC burst off-chip (is_tsc_pe). The fix: route each half's profiler burst to its own ht_tail via the proven block→ht_tail connect path (the z-handoff, region-scoped colors) and append it like the TSC burst.
  • Decode C is clean — measured early (during ingress); the reporter emits once, ht_tail appends it alongside its existing TSC burst; host adds one receive.
  • Prefill A/B has one wrinkle — measured in start_kv_transfer, after prefill's ht_tail already sent its logits blob + TSC burst → needs a second, later append (the one new bit to design).
  • Alt (single combined stream): carry prefill A/B north on the idle KV colors 17/21 through the seam into decode after the north-shift, collect A/B+C in decode, emit through the one decode logits egress.
